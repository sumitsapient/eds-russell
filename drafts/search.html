How to Test

  • Type 2+ characters -- watch autocomplete suggestions appear
  • Press ↓ / ↑ arrows to navigate suggestions
  • Press Enter with a suggestion highlighted -- navigate to that page
  • Click Search button or press Enter -- full results shown
  • Check the URL -- it updates to ?q=your-search-term
  • Share or bookmark the URL -- search results are preserved
  • Open DevTools Network -- look for /query-index.json request (once, cached)
  • Open DevTools Console with ?rum=on -- look for sampleRUM "search" event
